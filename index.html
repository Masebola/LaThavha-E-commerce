About Lathavha

Founded by Phalanndwa Thavhaziawa Sikhwivhilu, Lathavha is a proudly local business built on passion, hard work, and a commitment to quality.

Phalanndwa, an ambitious entrepreneur with deep roots in his community, began his journey selling fresh produce and essential goods to neighbors and nearby towns. Over the years, his vision grew — to create a business that not only offers high-quality products, but also supports local farmers, suppliers, and families.

Today, Lathavha provides a range of goods, from fresh, wholesome foods like mashontsa, achaar, and locally sourced meats, to trusted home and personal care products. Every product is carefully selected to ensure customers receive the best value and quality.
